# The Future of Design Ethics

As we integrate more machine learning and automated systems into our daily lives, the role of the designer is shifting from creating interfaces to designing ethical frameworks.

In my recent work with the **Master in Experience Design**, we've been exploring how game-making can serve as a powerful tool for students to "play out" ethical dilemmas. By creating rules and systems, students can see the consequences of their design choices in a safe, simulated environment.

## Key Takeaways:
- Ethics shouldn't be a separate module; it should be baked into the prototyping process.
- We need more "friction" in design to encourage reflection.
- The goal is not just "user-friendly" but "human-centric" and "society-aware".
