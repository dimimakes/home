# Smart Cities or Sustainable Communities?

The concept of the "Smart City" often focuses too much on the "Smart" (technology) and not enough on the "City" (people and community).

Through the **REBEL** programme, we are investigating how to re-imagine future smart living. It's not just about sensors and data; it's about local values and know-how. 

We need to move from "designing for" to "designing with". This means creating platforms where citizens can actively shape their environment, rather than just being data points in a lab.
